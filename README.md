The Customer Relationship Management In Vehicle Automobiles is designed to automate and simplify the sales process in automobile dealerships. The system maintains detailed records of customer enquiries, including their personal details, contact information, and the vehicle models they are interested in. It also manages the assignment of sales consultants who are responsible for handling customer interactions and follow-ups.
In addition, the system tracks the final stage of the sales process, known as retail or vehicle delivery, where the vehicle is delivered to the customer and all related details are stored in the system. By maintaining all information in a structured digital format, the system allows easy retrieval of customer and sales data whenever required. This reduces paperwork, saves time for dealership staff, and minimizes the chances of human errors.
